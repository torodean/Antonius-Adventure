\chapter{\keyword{ADVENTURERS}}


\section{\keyword{ATTRIBUTES}}

Every \keyword{ADVENTURER} has \keyword{ATTRIBUTES}. The \keyword{ATTRIBUTES} determine the stats of the specific \keyword{ADVENTURER} and provide improvements and different capabilities when doing \keyword{MISSIONS}.

\subsection{\keyword{STRENGTH}}

\keyword{STRENGTH} represents the strength of an adventurer. This value will affect the success rate of all \keyword{MISSIONS} but has a greater affect on \keyword{BATTLE MISSIONS}, \keyword{RESCUE MISSIONS}, \keyword{ESCORT MISSIONS}, and \keyword{HUNTING MISSIONS}.

\subsection{\keyword{STAMINA}}

\keyword{STAMINA} represents the Stamina and Vitality of an adventurer. This value will affect the success rate of all \keyword{MISSIONS} but has a greater affect on longer \keyword{MISSIONS}, \keyword{EXPLORATION MISSIONS}, \keyword{MINING MISSIONS} and \keyword{SURVIVAL MISSIONS}.

\subsection{\keyword{AGILITY}}

\keyword{AGILITY} represents the Agility and Speed of an adventurer. This value will affect the success rate of all \keyword{MISSIONS} but has a greater affect on shorter \keyword{MISSIONS}, \keyword{DELIVERY MISSIONS}, \keyword{STEALTH MISSIONS}, and \keyword{SABOTAGE MISSIONS}.

\subsection{\keyword{INTELLIGENCE}}

\keyword{INTELLIGENCE} represents the Intelligence and Knowledge of an adventurer. This value will affect the success rate of all \keyword{MISSIONS} but has a greater affect on \keyword{TRACKING MISSIONS}, \keyword{RESEARCH MISSIONS}, \keyword{STRATEGIC MISSIONS}, and \keyword{TECHNICAL MISSIONS}.

\subsection{\keyword{WISDOM}}

\keyword{WISDOM} represents the Wisdom and Intuition of an adventurer. This value will affect the success rate of all \keyword{MISSIONS} but has a greater affect on \keyword{DIPLOMATIC MISSIONS}, \keyword{SPIRITUAL MISSIONS}, \keyword{SUPPORT MISSIONS}, and improves the chance of getting more rewards for completing any \keyword{MISSION}.
